2. Select Source
Choose any app, stream, or system audio as input. Preview live before recording.

3. Enhance and Record
Apply VST/AudioUnit effects. Hit record—audio saves automatically in high quality.

4. Export and Enjoy
Access files instantly. Share, edit, or archive your enhanced audio.
